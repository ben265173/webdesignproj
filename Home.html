About this website

Student meals is the place for college students to go to for recipes and tips for cooking at home. Whether it is for breakfast, lunch, or dinner, there's a recipe for everything. You can also find the prices for a lot of the meals you will find (please not that prices are only an estimate) so you can easily find something within your budget. Want to recommend some recipel free to contact us in the "Contact Us"" section!

Why was it created?

This website was created with the thought of new college students being away from home and having to prepare meals for themselves. Students can sometimes have trouble with doing so as food can be expensive and they might not be used to cooking at home. This website was made to guide these students on meals which are easy to cook and also affordable.
